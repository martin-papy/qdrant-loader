This privacy policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit the QDrant Loader documentation website. We are committed to being transparent about our simple data practices.

What This Website Is

This is a static documentation website for QDrant Loader, an open-source Python project. We provide documentation, guides, and information about the project. We do not collect personal information, create user accounts, or store any data about you.

Analytics Data We Collect

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our documentation. This helps us improve the content and user experience. Google Analytics automatically collects:

  • Page views: Which pages you visit and how long you spend reading them
  • Basic device info: Browser type, operating system, and screen size (to ensure our site works well)
  • General location: Country or region (not your exact location)
  • How you found us: Whether you came from a search engine, GitHub, or another website
Important: This data is anonymous and aggregated. We cannot identify individual visitors.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies only for Google Analytics. We do not create our own cookies for user preferences, sessions, or any other purpose.

Cookie Source Purpose Duration
Google Analytics Track website usage for improvement Up to 2 years

Your Privacy Choices

You can control analytics tracking in several ways:

  • Consent Banner: When you first visit, you can accept or decline analytics tracking
  • Change Settings: Use the "Your Privacy Choices" link in our footer to modify your preferences anytime
  • Browser Settings: Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Google Analytics Opt-out: Install Google's opt-out browser add-on

What We Don't Do

We keep it simple:
  • ❌ We don't collect personal information like names, emails, or addresses
  • ❌ We don't create user accounts or profiles
  • ❌ We don't store any data about you on our servers
  • ❌ We don't sell or share any data with third parties (except Google Analytics)
  • ❌ We don't track you across other websites
  • ❌ We don't send marketing emails or communications

Third-Party Services

Our website uses these external services:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage statistics. View Google's Privacy Policy
  • Consent Management Platform: To manage your cookie preferences and comply with privacy laws
  • CDN Services: We load Bootstrap, icons, and other assets from content delivery networks for faster loading

International Visitors

This website is hosted and operated from various locations. Google Analytics data may be processed in the United States and other countries where Google operates. By using our website, you consent to this processing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Since we only collect basic analytics data, changes will likely be minimal.
